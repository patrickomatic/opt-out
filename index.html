Privacy workflow tool

Opt-Out Desk

Opt-Out Desk is a browser-based privacy checklist for manually finding and removing your personal information from data broker and people-search sites.

  • Track manual opt-out steps for common broker removal flows.
  • Generate discovery searches for phone, address, and name variants.
  • Keep profile details and progress local to your browser.
  • Export your progress as JSON and recheck listings later.

The app runs entirely on GitHub Pages and stores data only in browser localStorage under optOutDeskState.v2.
